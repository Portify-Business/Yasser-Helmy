Yasser Helmy

CEO of Papers for Trading & Contracting

  • Yaser Helmy is a distinguished professional with a robust career in the technology and business sectors. He holds a Bachelor's degree in Education from Helwan University, completed in 1999, and a Business Administration and Marketing diploma from Cairo University, attained in 2006.
  • In 2014, Yaser founded Papers Trading and Contracting, a private company specializing in trading building materials such as concrete, wood, sand, and block, as well as contracting services. Under his leadership, the company has achieved remarkable milestones, expanding its market presence and establishing a reputation for excellence and reliability.
  • Yaser's commitment to continuous learning, leadership excellence, and deep understanding of the technological and construction landscapes have been pivotal in his career. His journey from a software engineer to a CEO reflects his dedication, strategic vision, and ability to adapt to evolving industry demands.
  • In addition to his professional endeavors, Yaser is passionate about mentoring young professionals and contributing to community initiatives. He remains an active participant in industry forums, sharing insights and fostering collaborations that drive technological and business advancements.
